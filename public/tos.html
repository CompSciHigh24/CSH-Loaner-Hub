In order to borrow a loaner, a student will have to go to this website and locate the form. They can submit their information into a form to borrow a loaner. The form will be sent to the Ops Team and then the student can go to the main office to pick up the loaner.

To return a loaner, a student will simply go to the main office with their loaner to sign out the loaner. The Ops Team will then go to the admin page on the website and verify that the student signed out the loaner.

  • Borrowing a loaner too many times will result in you being banned from borrowing any loaners. You will have to speak to Mr P. before borrowing another loaner.
  • After filling out the loaner form and dropping off your chromebook, your phone can be picked up at the Main Office at the end of the day.
